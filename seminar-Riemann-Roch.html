Online Seminar on Riemann-Roch Theorem

I would like to organize an online seminar on Riemann-Roch theorem in June, 2020, which would be a discussion of appendix C in Mcduff and Salamon's book [1].

1. Prerequisites

Prerequisites for this seminar would be basic knowledges about functional analysis, especially the theory of Fredholm operators and Sobolev spaces. Knowledges on algebraic topology are also required, at least the computation methods that we could use to compute the fundamental group of matrix groups. Some familiarity with differential geometry is also required, since we will use connections and Hodge-star operators and things like that. People who did not familiar with these can refer to [3], [2] and [4].
